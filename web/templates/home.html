About Us

The St. Antoine Scout Group (SASG) is a community-focused organization that strives to instill values of leadership, discipline, and spirituality among its members. With a strong commitment to service, we aim to develop the character and skills of young people through outdoor activities, service projects, and leadership development programs.

Our Activities

We offer a wide range of activities including camping, hiking, community service, leadership workshops, and spiritual retreats. Join us to be part of a vibrant community that values growth and teamwork!
